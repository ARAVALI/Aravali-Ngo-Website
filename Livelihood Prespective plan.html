ARAVALI provides support to its partner organisation to build perspective of the organisation on livelihood intervention in their operational area. This gives the partner organisations’ an understanding on the following:

  • Existing livelihoods opportunities of the communities.
  • Identify potential livelihood options, with specific focus on the poorest and women
  • Identify resources (including human, financial and time frame) for livelihood interventions in their work area.
  • Identify possible constraints that the organisation is likely to face while initiating these potential livelihood activities.

........................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................

Facilitating the process of preparation of LPP is intended to:-

  • LPP to serve as the guiding and strategy document for the partner NGO to initiate work on community focused livelihood enhancement interventions.
  • To help partner organisation plan and implement a programme for rural livelihood enhancement in its area of operation.
  • To build the understanding of partner organisations related to livelihood enhancement
  • To develop an in-depth “understanding of the existing livelihoods activities of the target communities and identify potential options, with particular focus on the poorest and women.

The process of preparation of Livelihood Perspective Plan (LLP) of partner organizations are undertaken by them with the process facilitation by ARAVALI and finally also helping the organization write the plan.
